About Me

My name is Jenny O'Donnell.

I'm a second year student at the University of Florida with a major in public relations and a minor in nonprofit leadership and management. I'm hoping to combine my passions for in-depth storytelling, multimedia, and international mission work in my professional career.

I've exlplored these areas of interest throughout my time in college. Whether traveling with international students, creating and maintaing my blogs, or helping unerprivaleged children, I always try to find inspiration through all my endeavors.

My love of writing and storytelling formed when I was a child. I would make up stories in my head before I went to bed, and fervently right them down before I could forget anything (or before my parents came in and yelled at me for being awake).

Now, this passion has evolved into something more. I used to think the best stories were ones made purely from imagination; now, I know that they are born from truth. People who you think may lead ordinary lives can have the most poignant stories behind them.

On this website, you will find links and media that showcase my work and inspirations. I hope that you find as much inspiration behind these stories as I do.
