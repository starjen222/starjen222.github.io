This page is dedicated to my endeavors with UF Christian Campus House.

My journey of faith led me to this organization last year. I felt as though I was unfulfilled and longing for a greater aspect of family, of service, and of mission-oriented community.

Christian Campus House has reinvigorated that purpose within me and has provided me with some of the greatest opportunities for spiritual, relational, and professional growth.

My involvement at CCH has deepened tremendously since when I first joined. I came in as a passive observer, new to faith and with stereotypical views of what Christianity, church and religion were supposed to be.

Now, as a servant leader, director of service opportunities, and a future director of outreach, I see my involvement as a precursor to a career of missions, service, and creative communication.

Through my efforts of strategic communication across campus, dedicated mission work (whether abroad or right here in Gainesville), and passionate service, I have become part of what I like to call the new age of faith

Faith isn’t exclusion or hatred; faith is love and acceptance and reaching out to those in need; whether religion is a factor or not, I know that this is where I need to be

I made this video in combination with a story about two different outreach opportunities that I’m involved with at Christian Campus House: Downtown Outreach and Christian Campus House.
