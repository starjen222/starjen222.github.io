Bays Mountain fire tower

For Martin Luther King, Jr. weekend, some friends and I traveled to Tennessee to go hiking and skiing in Bays Mountain Park. It was such a long hike to get to this tower, which was made of old steel and wood and seemed to shudder in the wind. The view peaking thourgh the empty steel bars was terrifying...but beautiful.
